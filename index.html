The Role

This is a role for someone who sees a home as a living system.

"You walk into a home and immediately know what needs to happen next. You reset spaces without being asked. You create structure where things used to feel reactive. You take pride in laundry done correctly, kitchens that flow, and homes that return to calm every single day."

Daily Responsibilities

What You'll Be Doing Each Day

01

Daily Reset

Kitchen, common areas, bedrooms as needed — returning every space to calm

02

Laundry Management

Wash, fold, put away (correctly) — with care and attention to detail

03

Organization Maintenance

Home already has systems; you help maintain and improve them

04

Inventory Awareness

Restocking pantry, fridge, supplies, and household goods proactively

05

Errands & Prep

Grocery runs, household pickups, weekly lunches, protein shakes, and ingredients

06

Vendor Coordination

Coordinate cleaners, repairs, services, and vehicle maintenance

Ideal Candidate

You Are Someone Who...

Notices the small things because you know they're never actually small.

Extremely Detail-Oriented

Observant and thorough in everything you do

Naturally Proactive

Handling things before they're asked

Independent & Quiet

Comfortable working without needing oversight

Highly Trustworthy

Respectful of boundaries and confidentiality

Excellent Communicator

Closing loops and sharing progress

Hospitality-Minded

A true "hotel reset" standard for the home

You take pride in how a space feels, not just how it looks.

Requirements

Experience & Fit

1+ years of household management experience, family assistant experience, or similar home-operations role

Experience supporting families with school-aged children (parenting experience is a plus, but not required)

Comfortable working in a multi-ethnic household and handling a variety of foods

No food-handling restrictions (must be comfortable with nuts, shellfish, etc.)

Physically capable of active mornings and household movement

Authorized to work in the US
